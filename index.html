F.A.Q

Essential Driver Training (EDT) is a mandatory training course for learner drivers to help improve driving skills and safety.For your lessons, please bring your provisional driving license. It's also helpful to bring glasses or contact lenses if you require them for driving.
Book via Phone Call, Text, or WhatsApp We'll respond promptly to arrange your lesson
We understand that circumstances can change. We require 48 hours' notice for cancellations to avoid charges. For cancellations with less than 48 hours' notice, we charge 50% of the lesson fee. No-shows or same-day cancellations are charged at the full lesson rate
To be eligible for a driving test in Ireland, you must complete 12 Essential Driver Training (EDT) lessons with an RSA-approved driving instructor. These lessons are mandatory for all learner drivers. While 12 lessons are the minimum, many learners take additional lessons to build confidence and refine their skills
